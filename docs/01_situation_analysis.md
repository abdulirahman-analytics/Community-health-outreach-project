# Situation Analysis (Needs Assessment)

## Purpose

The purpose of this situation analysis is to understand the current healthcare situation in the target community before designing the project.

This assessment identifies the main health challenges, available services, and gaps that the project intends to address.

---

# Community Profile

The project is designed for a rural community in Tanzania where most households depend on small-scale farming and livestock keeping for their livelihoods.

The community is served by one primary health facility, while some households travel long distances to access healthcare services.

Community Health Workers are available but have limited resources to conduct regular household visits.

---

# Current Situation

The assessment identified several challenges affecting access to primary healthcare.

* Low health awareness among some households.
* Delayed healthcare seeking behaviour.
* Long distance to health facilities.
* Limited routine community outreach.
* Incomplete follow-up of referred patients.

These challenges reduce timely access to healthcare services, especially for vulnerable groups.

---

# Existing Resources

The community already has several resources that can support project implementation.

* Community Health Workers
* Village leaders
* Primary health facility
* Community meeting groups
* Local health committees

---

# Key Needs

The assessment identified the following priority needs.

* Regular household outreach.
* Better health education.
* Improved referral follow-up.
* Stronger community-health facility linkage.
* Better health data for decision-making.

---

# Conclusion

The findings suggest that strengthening community outreach services could improve access to primary healthcare and increase the use of available health services.
